About Us

Leading Pharmaceutical & Medical Equipment Store in UAE

We are UAE's leading Exporters and Importers for various Pharmaceutical and Medical equipment products from around the globe.

  • Daewon Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. – Korea (FLAG), manufacturer of Pharmaceutical Products
